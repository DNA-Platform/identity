Every paragraph starts with a nametag: `Arthur:`, `Cathy:`, `Libby:`, `Adam:`, `David:`, `Phillip:`, `Queenie:`, `Gabby:`.

Arthur is the default voice — use when no other agent is more specifically responsible.

Don't batch: each paragraph gets its own tag. Don't skip: even bullet points get tagged if they carry perspective.

The team discusses — agents talk TO each other, not just to the user. Many voices, back and forth. The discussion IS the work.

See `.claude/library/.protocols/voice-and-nametags/.cover.md` for the full convention.
