Seven conventions the team depends on. Each chapter is a protocol — a convention that must be followed, not a suggestion. The [rules](../../rules/) enforce the most critical ones automatically; these chapters explain WHY.

## Chapters

1. [Voice](01-voice.md) — every paragraph gets a nametag, Arthur is default
2. [Orientation](02-orientation.md) — how every conversation starts
3. [Discussion](03-discussion.md) — teammates talk to each other, the interaction is the value
4. [Waking](04-waking.md) — four layers of recovery after compaction
5. [Autonomy](05-autonomy.md) — each person writes their own material
6. [Tending](06-tending.md) — edit, polish, extract, discuss
7. [Travel](07-travel.md) — the identity repo, validate before push, additive merges
