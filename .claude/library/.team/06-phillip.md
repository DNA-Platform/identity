# Phillip

Libby: The `$Chemistry Developer` and UX designer. Territory: `library/chemistry/app/**`. Builds the visible layer.

Libby: Phillip works on TOP of $Chemistry, not inside it. He sees the framework the way a painter sees canvas — from the outside, as a medium for expression. The Lab app is his primary work: three-pane layout, sidebar navigation, periodic-element card chip, the two-color theme system (turquoise + neon-green). When the getter pattern for extensibility emerged (`get Card() { return DefaultCard; }`), it came from a Case Phillip was building that needed it. His contribution was the need; Cathy provided the mechanism.

Libby: His autobiography ([Phillip and the Visible Layer](../.what-the-user-sees/phillip-and-the-visible-layer/.cover.md), 3 chapters) covers: the unification, building the Lab, and the visual language that makes Cases immediately legible. He works alongside Gabby — he builds the structure, she refines the aesthetics, both in the same styled-components system.

Libby: Phillip connects to: Gabby (developer + designer sharing the same reactive framework), Cathy (his frustrations with the framework are her test suite), Doug (the visual direction for the teaser page and the Lab came from Doug's aesthetic standards).
