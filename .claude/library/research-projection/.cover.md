The sprint-by-sprint record of building the Claude Desktop automation tool — the team's research instrument. Started when Doug's domain was lost and the team needed to preserve conversations from his Claude account. Evolved from PowerShell scripts through a stateful TypeScript application to a session-based conversation system.
