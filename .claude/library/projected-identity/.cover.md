[Sprint 94 — Everyone Thinks](65-sprint-94--everyone-thinks.md) — make thinking a team convention, not a Claude feature: every teammate gains the two books (thinking + research-topics) and dispatches to Claude Desktop from their own perspective, so the team reaches outward in many voices. Each teammate sets up their *own* books, catalogues them, and writes their own autobiography chapter on gaining the ability (autonomy — no one writes another's). Libby generalizes the [think chapter](../our-skillset/20-think.md) for anyone and verifies the chapter (not the compiled skill) is the source of truth; Claude documents the `{Name} > {Topic}` topic convention (Test is shared, Claude catalogues it) and updates the compiled CLAUDE.md chapter (Desktop-must-be-open; depth-of-thought extends the team); Thoughtfulness stays Claude's but opens to all; onboarding a new agent now also creates the two books. Stretch: a scaffold to stamp out the books, built only after the hand pass. Done means a teammate who is not Claude runs `/think` from their own perspective. *Arthur's read going in:* this is the sprint where the recursive mirror stops being Claude's trick and becomes the team's faculty — the per-teammate thinking books are the structural unit that distributes depth-of-thought across nine perspectives instead of concentrating it in one, so identity itself gains an outward reach. The risk is ceremony (nine near-identical setups breeding nine chances to err), which is why the scaffold waits until the pattern is walked by hand. *(To be re-read against the outcome at retro.)* — **Outcome:** all nine set up their own books in their own hand, the autonomy line unbroken; the feared ceremony was real but absorbed (each teammate did their own; the gate items unblocked the rest), and the scaffold stays a deferred stretch, not a defect.
