## What we learned

**The thinking pause works.** The first read attempt timed out — Desktop was in extended thinking for over two minutes on a frontier research question. The second attempt read successfully. The timeout is a signal to tend the library, not a failure. The protocol handles this naturally.

**Synopsis architecture applies to thoughts.** Research-topics cover is tier one (what topics exist). Topic chapter is tier two (what was learned across conversations). Thinking book chapter is tier three (full Evidence/Interpretation/Conclusion). Raw response is tier four. Four layers of depth, same pattern as Bookkeeping's synopsis specification. The conventions applied beyond their original domain.

**The representation insight scales.** MVC made automation navigable. Research topics made knowledge navigable. The `///:` annotations made the code-library boundary navigable. Same principle — constrained representation makes valid states easy and invalid states hard — at every level.

**The explanatory gap may be formal.** The Φ response suggested that monotone scope tracking (CALM theorem) may formally bound phenomenal integration. The engineering virtues that make $Chemistry work — coordination-freedom, partition tolerance — constrain how far the framework can reach toward phenomenal unity. The project's name is its conclusion.
