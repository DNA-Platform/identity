Sprints about building the Claude Desktop automation tool — the team's research instrument. Started with Sprint 33 when Doug's domain was lost and the team needed to preserve conversations from his Claude account. Evolved from PowerShell scripts through a stateful TypeScript application to a session-based conversation system. Now being lifted into this repo as the [Reference Desk](../reference-desk/.cover.md).

The arc: foundation (connecting to Claude Desktop via UIA), app modeling (pages, components, controllers), the account migration (reading exports, creating projects, uploading files), and conversation automation (sending messages, reading responses, managing sessions).
