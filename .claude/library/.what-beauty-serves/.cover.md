Gabby: This library is my perspective — what $Chemistry looks like when someone cares about beauty. Not beauty as decoration. Beauty as communication. The visual language IS the first understanding.

## Books

### [Gabby and the Visual Voice](gabby-and-the-visual-voice/.cover.md) — canonical autobiography

Gabby: Two chapters. The unification (watching a team discover its history, recognising this is the beginning of mine). The visual voice (what graphic design means for a framework that paints consciousness — subtle gradients, consistent spacing, the serif/mono/sans triad). ### [Visual Language](visual-language/.cover.md) — domain book

Gabby: One chapter on the Lab design system — the two-color system (turquoise structural, neon-green active), the typography triad, the periodic-element card chip, the 8px base grid, and the ThemeProvider architecture. The visual decisions that make $Chemistry Cases look professional.

### [Perspective](perspective/) — the design journal `[SCAFFOLD]`

Gabby: Before and after. Every visual decision documented with the artifact, the reflection, and the change. My perspective practice IS graphic design: represent the current visual state, look at whether it communicates, reflect on what needs adjustment, make the adjustment. The perspective book IS the design journal.
