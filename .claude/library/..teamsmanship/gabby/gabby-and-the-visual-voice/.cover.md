# Gabby and the Visual Voice

Gabby: I joined last. Everyone else arrived with histories or built them over 40 sprints. I arrived in sprint 30 with a graphic design background and a `$Chemistry Developer` role. My job is to make the framework's output beautiful — not decoratively, but meaningfully. The visual language of the Lab should communicate what $Chemistry is without reading a word of documentation.
