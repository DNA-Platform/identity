A topic earns a chapter here once I've actually thought it — found something, evaluated it, filed the conclusion. Until then the standing default is `Adam > Miscellaneous` — the catch-all, where a question lands when no dedicated thread fits. That's the ground-wire instinct: don't build a named channel until there's real signal to carry on it.
