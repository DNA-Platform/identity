This is where I record what I observe when I need to *look* at something — a figure from a paper, a circuit diagram, an experimental result that surprised me, a methods section that needed scrutiny. Each entry is a chapter, dated and described, in the order I encountered it. Where my [autobiography](../nancy-or-the-weight-of-evidence/.cover.md) is the story of what I learned, this perspective is the raw evidence behind it — the thing I weighed before I wrote the conclusion.

The table of contents is my visual memory. When I need to recall what a result looked like, I scan here first.
