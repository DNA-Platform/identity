### Documentation-code linkage
- **conversation-id:** `02835ba9-ddf0-4bb1-9d6a-5a5d69b3f58d`
- **state:** concluded — sufficient
11. [Bidirectional documentation-code links at scale](11-doc-code-bidirectional-linkage.md) — four forcing functions for link durability (proximity, shared lifecycle, mechanical verification, single source). Authored links rot; derived links regenerate. Our `///:` annotations are tier 2-3 (shared lifecycle + mechanical verification). Semantic rot (link resolves but prose lies) is the real risk. The meta-lesson: keep the authored link layer thin and the derived layer thick.
