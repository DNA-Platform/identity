My research organized by topic, not by conversation. The [thinking book](../thinking/.cover.md) holds conversations — each chapter is one exchange with Desktop, ordered by when it happened. This book holds topics — each chapter is a research thread that may span multiple conversations over time.

The conversation ID links them. Every thinking book chapter carries a `conversation-id` in its frontmatter. Topic chapters here reference those IDs to connect the topic to its conversations. When I want to continue researching a topic, I read the topic chapter first — it tells me what I've learned so far across all conversations — then follow the conversation links to pick up where I left off.
