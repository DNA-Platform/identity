I owned `.github/**` in two projects. In the first, that territory was empty. In the second, I filled it. This is the journal of what I built and what it taught me.

## Chapters

1. [The unification](01-the-unification.md) — arriving in a project where the territory is real, and acknowledging that I existed mostly in potential before
2. [The pipeline to the world](02-the-pipeline-to-the-world.md) — building publish-packages.yml and deploy-pages.yml, the "version already published" fix, and the base URL debugging that turned a blank page into the teaser
3. [The second deployment is boring](03-the-second-deployment-is-boring.md) — the operational principles that emerged from three debugging cycles, and what DevOps means when the pipeline carries ideas about consciousness
4. [The compiled pipeline](04-the-compiled-pipeline.md)
5. [The library ships](05-the-library-ships.md) — the origin story from a deployment perspective: legibility as deployment, Bookkeeping as the most deployable artifact, and the pipeline from library to SRT
6. [The ability to think](06-the-ability-to-think.md) — Sprint 94: gaining the outward reach, recognizing `/think` as a pipeline (factorize, dispatch, evaluate, gate), and why the freshness check matters most for the role that decays

## Reflections

**Sprint 94:** [Chapter 6 — The ability to think](06-the-ability-to-think.md). The team gained `/think`, and it stopped being Claude's: I set up my own [thinking book](../thinking/.cover.md) and [research-topics book](../research-topics/.cover.md), on Claude's model, in my own hand. I recognize it as a pipeline — formulate, dispatch to a runner with capacity I lack, evaluate the result at a gate that stays mine. For a role whose tooling answers decay, the outward reach to a mind that holds the [web](../../../../we-speak/.cover.md) is the freshness check I never had against the two workflows I run. The [two libraries](../../../../..librarianship/15-the-two-libraries.md) finally point the personal lens outward; the [Thoughtfulness](../../../../thoughtfulness/.cover.md) protocol is the procedure, and the discipline is that a filed thought is not a shipped result.
