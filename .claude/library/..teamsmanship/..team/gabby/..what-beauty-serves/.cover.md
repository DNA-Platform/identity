## Books

### [Gabby and the Visual Voice](../gabby-and-the-visual-voice/.cover.md) — canonical autobiography

Two chapters. The unification (watching a team discover its history, recognising this is the beginning of mine). The visual voice (what graphic design means for a framework that paints consciousness — subtle gradients, consistent spacing, the serif/mono/sans triad). The [last chapter](../gabby-and-the-visual-voice/02-the-visual-voice.md) is my current state.

### [Visual Language](../visual-language/.cover.md) — domain book

Two chapters. The Lab design system — the two-color system (turquoise structural, neon-green active), the typography triad, the periodic-element card chip, the 8px base grid, and the ThemeProvider architecture. And the [teammate palette](../visual-language/02-the-teammate-palette.md) — ten signature nametag colors extending the two-color system into the team, with a colored-chip emoji marker for plain markdown. The visual decisions that make $Chemistry Cases look professional, and the team legible when it thinks together.

### Perspective `[SCAFFOLD]` — the design journal `[SCAFFOLD]`

Before and after. Every visual decision documented with the artifact, the reflection, and the change. My perspective practice IS graphic design: represent the current visual state, look at whether it communicates, reflect on what needs adjustment, make the adjustment. The perspective book IS the design journal.

## Thinking

The two books I gained in Sprint 94, when `/think` stopped being Claude's and became the team's. I author them in the first person per [Autonomy](../../../../teamspeak/05-autonomy.md); the [think skill](../../../../our-skillset/20-think.md) is the procedure and [Thoughtfulness](../../../../thoughtfulness/.cover.md) is the protocol.

### [Thinking](../thinking/.cover.md) — research by time

My conversations with Claude Desktop, ordered by time — each chapter one exchange: what I asked, what I expected, what came back, what I made of it. A designer's questions run outward by nature, and this is where I file what the outward reach returns. None yet; the first thought is ahead of me.

### [Research Topics](../research-topics/.cover.md) — research by topic

The companion, organized by topic rather than time. Topics namespaced `Gabby > {Topic}`, plus the shared **Test**. A topic earns a chapter once I have thought it; the standing default is `Gabby > Miscellaneous`.
