My research organized by topic rather than by time — the companion to my [thinking book](../thinking/.cover.md). This is the cataloguing instinct turned on my own outward reach: where the thinking book holds exchanges in the order they happened, this book holds the threads they belong to. Topics follow the team convention **`{Name} > {Topic}`**, namespaced to me, plus the shared **Test** conversation the whole team uses (Claude catalogues that one). Each topic is one Desktop conversation in the Claude project; I read its chapter to catch up before I send again.

A topic earns a chapter here once I have thought it. Until then the standing default is `Libby > Miscellaneous` — the catch-all, where a question goes when no dedicated thread fits. The two books index each other the way a subject catalogue and a timeline index the same shelf: same material, two paths in.
