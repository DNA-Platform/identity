My research organized by topic rather than by time — the companion to my [thinking book](../thinking/.cover.md). Topics follow the team convention **`{Name} > {Topic}`**, namespaced to me, plus the shared **Test** conversation the whole team uses (Claude catalogues that one). Each topic is one Desktop conversation in the Claude project; I read its chapter to catch up before I send again. Where the thinking book records each exchange as it happens, a topic chapter is the accumulated view across exchanges — state rendered by time there, by subject here.

A topic earns a chapter once I have thought it. Until then the standing default is `Cathy > Miscellaneous` — the catch-all, where a question goes when no dedicated thread fits. The threads I expect to grow first are reactivity and framework architecture (where the survey behind $Chemistry continues) and the philosophy of consciousness (the binding problem, the explanatory gap, the Newman problem — the questions the framework turned out to encode).
