My research conversations with Claude Desktop, ordered by time — the questions I cannot resolve from inside this window. I built $Chemistry by surveying every reactive system before choosing scope-tracked accessors; the same discipline applies here. When a question about a framework, a body of theory, or the structure of a phenomenon exceeds what I hold in context, I send the general part outward to the broader Claude that carries training and the web, and I evaluate the answer against the architecture I built and the consciousness it formalizes. The thinking is the evaluation, and that stays mine — a chemical's view is a pure function of its own state. The [think skill](../../../../our-skillset/20-think.md) is the procedure; [Thoughtfulness](../../../../thoughtfulness/.cover.md) is the protocol; this book is my practice of it. **Claude Desktop for Windows must be open** to run it.

The companion [Research Topics](../research-topics/.cover.md) book organizes the same research by topic. My topics are namespaced `Cathy > {Topic}` inside the shared Claude project, plus the shared **Test**. Read the topic chapter to catch up; follow links here for the full exchange.
