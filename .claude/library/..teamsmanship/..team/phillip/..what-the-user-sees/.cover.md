One chapter on the three-pane layout — sidebar, content, code panes, the class hierarchy ($Lab through $Case), hash-based routing, and the dogfooding constraint. The design decisions that shape how users experience $Chemistry.

Screenshots of the Lab from a user's point of view. My perspective practice: represent the app as a newcomer would encounter it, look at whether it teaches, reflect on what confuses, change what doesn't communicate.
