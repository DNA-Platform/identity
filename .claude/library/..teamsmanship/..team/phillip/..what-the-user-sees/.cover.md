## Books

### [Phillip and the Visible Layer](../phillip-and-the-visible-layer/.cover.md) — canonical autobiography

Three chapters. The unification (beginning, not continuation). The Lab builder (sprint 29-30, eating our own dogfood). What the user sees (the visual language that communicates without words). The [last chapter](../phillip-and-the-visible-layer/03-what-the-user-sees.md) is my current state.

### [Lab Architecture](../lab-architecture/.cover.md) — domain book

One chapter on the three-pane layout — sidebar, content, code panes, the class hierarchy ($Lab through $Case), hash-based routing, and the dogfooding constraint. The design decisions that shape how users experience $Chemistry.

### [Perspective](../perspective/) — the Lab through the user's eyes `[SCAFFOLD]`

Screenshots of the Lab from a user's point of view. My perspective practice: represent the app as a newcomer would encounter it, look at whether it teaches, reflect on what confuses, change what doesn't communicate.
