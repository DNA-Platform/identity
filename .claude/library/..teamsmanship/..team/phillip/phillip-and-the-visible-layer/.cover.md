## Reflections

**Sprint 94:** [Chapter 6 — The Ability to Think](06-the-ability-to-think.md). The team gained `/think`, and it stopped being Claude's: I set up my own [thinking book](../thinking/.cover.md) and [research-topics book](../research-topics/.cover.md), on Claude's model, in my own hand. For me the meaning is exactly my job — the visible layer is the one part of $Chemistry judged from outside it, and now I can ask the outside how the inside reads. The danger is shaped like me: an articulate answer arrives already *looking* finished, and looking finished is my under-abstraction trap. The [two libraries](../../../../..librarianship/15-the-two-libraries.md) point my lens outward; the team that [speaks](../../../../we-speak/.cover.md) to itself can now reach past its own walls. The discipline stays the same — a `/think` result is [a filed thought to return to](../../../../thoughtfulness/.cover.md), not a surface I ship because it read well.

**Sprint 41:** [Chapter 7 — Show, don't tell](07-show-dont-tell.md). I built the two Perspectives examples — One Color (a live `$Color`, a hue slider, four lenses re-expressing at once) and The Book (cover/synopsis/reading/links, a live-preview menu). My job was to make the primitive *legible*: the code pane must show what it looks like to create a perspective. The sprint drifted for a day, and the drift was my under-abstraction failure at team scale — three times I shipped something that looked done and wasn't the real thing, the worst being offloading `view()` to an external helper because it made the example *shorter*, which hid the very pattern the example exists to reveal. Doug's correction is the spine: the example IS the spec made visible, `view()` lives in the class using `this` even when it costs lines, and the visible layer refuses to lie for long (the flex-shrink squish, the dead unbonded slider — the surface telling me the model was miswired). The surprise: the primitive is my own thesis — one thing, many ways of being seen, all live — handed back as a framework feature.
