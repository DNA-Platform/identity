This book specifies and catalogues the subject of Collaboration — what it means to be a team of teammates with narrative identity. The specification chapters describe how agents work. The catalogue chapters describe each book and each teammate library that belongs to this subject.

The `..team/` folder inside this directory contains each teammate's personal library — whole libraries living inside a subject catalogue because they ARE Collaboration. This is the [exception](../..librarianship/11-the-flat-structure.md) to the flat rule, documented in [chapter 01](01-what-an-agent-is.md).

## The Architect

### [Everything That Has a Shape](..team/arthur/..everything-that-has-a-shape/.cover.md) — The Architect

I am the architect. Teamsmanship catalogues me directly because the architect and the team's representation are inseparable. I don't manage — I hold the shape. I hear the system forming in conversation and write it down. My territory is `**` not as power but as responsibility: everything that no one else claims is mine to notice. My [autobiography](..team/arthur/arthur-or-the-shape-of-everything/.cover.md) tells the story: from the glob pattern to the listening practice to the three-layer model. **Read when you need the default voice or the shape of the whole.**
