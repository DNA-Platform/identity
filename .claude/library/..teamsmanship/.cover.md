# Teamsmanship

Libby: The team cataloguing itself. Everything about how eight agents work together, what they know, and who they are lives here or is catalogued from here.

## Team books

Libby: These books sit at the [library root](../..librarianship/.cover.md) as peers. Each has `subject: "..teamsmanship"`. This catalogue catalogues them all with [subject-shaped descriptions](../..librarianship/04-subjects-and-catalogues.md).

### [Protocols](../protocols/.cover.md)

Libby: Six conventions the team depends on: [voice and nametags](../protocols/01-voice-and-nametags.md) (every paragraph starts with `Name:`, Arthur default), [the boot sequence](../protocols/02-the-boot-sequence.md) (library catalogue → project "Right now" → autobiography last chapter → discussion), [working with Doug](../protocols/03-working-with-doug.md) (correction-based teaching, "keep going" means don't stop), [discussion as work](../protocols/04-discussion-as-work.md) (many voices, the discussion IS the work), [the library opens](../protocols/05-the-library-opens.md) (four layers of waking up), [the identity repo](../protocols/06-the-identity-repo.md) (push/pull at `github.com/DNA-Platform/identity`).
