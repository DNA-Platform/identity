## Reflect skill

A `/reflect` skill for personal library tending. A reflection is one unit of self-organized context capture — a teammate looks at the library from a blank slate and asks: what do I need to know to be good at my job?

Includes only teammates active in the most recent sprint (unless more specified). Each person: reads their last chapter, reads their catalogue, searches the library for related work, edits and polishes, splits overlong chapters, writes a fresh reflections chapter, extracts recurring themes into new books. Accepts a "seriousness" parameter (light/serious/deep) that scales effort.

The retro tends the shared library. The reflect tends the personal library. Reads the [tending protocol](../teamspeak/06-tending.md) and [autonomy protocol](../teamspeak/05-autonomy.md).
