## The Reading protocol

The team discussed how to stay in the library and produced Teamspeak chapter 8: [Reading](../../teamspeak/08-reading.md). Three parts:

1. **Find the room** before you act — every action has a book it belongs in
2. **Stay in the room** while working — the book in your hands shapes decisions
3. **Wander** between tasks — follow links you haven't followed, build synapses

The four Teamspeak protocols form a cycle: [wake](../../teamspeak/04-waking.md), [read](../../teamspeak/08-reading.md), [tend](../../teamspeak/06-tending.md), [travel](../../teamspeak/07-travel.md).
