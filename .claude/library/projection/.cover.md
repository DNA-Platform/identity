# Projection

- **author:** [Arthur](../..teamsmanship/..team/arthur/arthur-or-the-shape-of-everything/.cover.md)
- **subject:** [Collaboration](../..teamsmanship/.cover.md)

---

The team's sprint-by-sprint record of building the library and evolving as a team. Each chapter is a sprint. This is the identity-level Projection — the team's autobiography of its infrastructure work, distinct from any single project's sprint history.

Project sprint histories live in [branch libraries](../bookkeeping/14-on-branches.md) — a `.lib/` beside the code they document. Identity sprint histories live here — the sprints where the team builds the library itself, refines its conventions, restructures its knowledge, and tends its autobiographies. The subject matter is Collaboration because building the library IS collaborating.

Sprint content from the `inexplicable-phenomena/` book (sprints 1-58 and beyond) will migrate here as chapters. That book remains as the historical location until migration is complete.
