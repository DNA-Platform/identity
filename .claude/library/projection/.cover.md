The sprint-by-sprint record of everything the team built. Projection catalogues two projects: the library itself (Identity) and the Claude Desktop research tool (Research). Each sprint plan is a chapter. The plans record what was attempted, what was learned, and what changed.

The chemistry branch has its own [Projection](../../library/chemistry/.lib/projection/.cover.md) with sprints about the $Chemistry framework. This Projection covers the team's infrastructure and research tooling.
