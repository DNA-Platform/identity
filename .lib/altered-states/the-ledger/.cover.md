# The Ledger

- **author:** [Libby](../../../.claude/library/..teamsmanship/..team/libby/libby-and-the-tended-garden/.cover.md)
- **subject:** [Altered States](../..altered-states/.cover.md)

The **aggregate of correctness** — one durable register of everything we have **verified or agreed**, and everything still **open**, so that thinking hard about a new hard thing never costs us the settled knowledge. Doug's rule, made a place: every entry carries its **source**, and an answer is either *confirmed* (read from the installed source, or agreed by the team) or *open* (a question, with the decision it awaits) — never a guess wearing the confidence of a fact.

This is deliberately distinct from its two neighbours. [The active-image run](../the-build/13-the-active-image-run.md) is the **live process** record — what is happening now, tagged as it moves. The [archive README](../../reports/dataset-archive/README.md) is the **clean final procedure** an outside scientist reads. *This* is the standing memory of **what is true and what is unsettled**, updated as answers land and questions arise. Read it first when a parameter or a method is in doubt: if it is in Confirmed, we know it — and how we know it; if it is in Open, do not assume it.
