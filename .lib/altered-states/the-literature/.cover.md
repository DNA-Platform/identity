The branch's record of the papers we stand on — what *we* learned from each, and what that learning informs. The papers themselves are full books in [`library/papers/`](../../papers/digital-twins-tolias-2022/.cover.md), each with its own synopsis, figures, and commentary; this catalogue does **not** duplicate them. Each chapter points to a paper's book and adds the one thing the paper book cannot hold: *our* reading of it — what we took, which of our code or reports it touched, and what is still pending. The purpose is memory: next time we open one of these papers, this is where we find what we already know about it and what it informed, so we do not relearn it from scratch.
