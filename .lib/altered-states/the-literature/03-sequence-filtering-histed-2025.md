## What it is

Two-photon **holographic optogenetic stimulation** showing that recurrent V1 networks **filter** input sequences — boosting patterns that arrive in their natural temporal context, attenuating those that don't — a predictive-processing reading of recurrent cortical dynamics.

## What it informs

**Not yet load-bearing for our analysis.** It is a conceptual neighbour — the mechanism a *release-hallucination* or structured-spontaneous-activity framing would ride on (see [The Altered Cortex](../the-altered-cortex/.cover.md)) — but it does not currently drive our methodology or our figures. A stub on purpose: expand it if and when the recurrent-dynamics angle becomes part of the work.
