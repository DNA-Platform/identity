The sprint-by-sprint record of the team's effort on altered-states — a psychedelic exploration into altered states of consciousness using mouse visual cortex as our lens. A projection maps one space onto another: sprints live in the team's identity, and Projection maps them onto this branch, recording what the team actually did here in the project's own terms. Each chapter is one sprint — what it aimed to do, what was built, and what was learned — and together they form the branch's autobiography. The last sprint chapter is always the current state. This table of contents is empty at creation and grows as sprints are written.
