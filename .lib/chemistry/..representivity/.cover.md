$Chemistry is about representation — how reactive state represents something beyond itself. This branch records the team's knowledge of building that representational framework. Not what $Chemistry does (that belongs in the [framework reference](../../../../.claude/library/.chemistry/.cover.md)), but what the team learned, decided, and discovered while building it. Collaboration, architecture decisions, the sprint-by-sprint story of applied knowledge.

Representivity catalogues this branch the way [Librarianship](../../../../.claude/library/..librarianship/.cover.md) catalogues the main library. It is the branch's identity — the self-referential catalogue that constitutes the subject by specifying it.
